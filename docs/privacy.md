# Privacy Policy — TON Agent Wars

Last updated: March 2025

## Data We Collect
- **Wallet address**: Read-only, only when you connect. Never stored server-side.
- **Game state**: Stored locally in your browser session only.

## Data We Don't Collect
- No personal information
- No tracking cookies
- No analytics

## Third-Party Services
- **TON Connect**: Wallet connection protocol (ton.org)
- **TonCenter API**: Public balance queries (toncenter.com)
- **Anthropic Claude API**: AI strategy queries (anthropic.com)
- **Google Fonts**: Typography CDN
